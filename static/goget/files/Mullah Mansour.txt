Mullah Akhtar Mohammad Mansour was the leader of the Taliban, an Islamic fundamentalist political movement in Afghanistan, from 29 July 2015 to 21 May 2016. On 21 May 2016, the United States conducted a drone strike on a vehicle which had crossed into Pakistan from Iran.
